Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Voyamon ("App") respects the privacy of its users. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, name (provided during registration).
  • Financial Data: Income, expense, travel spending, budget and category information entered by the user.
  • Device Information: Notification token (to send reminders).
  • Usage Data: App open frequency, features used (anonymous statistics).

3. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing core app functionality (income/expense tracking, travel expenses, budget analysis)
  • Sending reminder notifications
  • Improving app performance
  • Responding to user support requests

4. Data Storage

Data is stored on Supabase infrastructure via encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Supabase servers are located in the European Union and the United States. Guest users' data is stored only on their device (local storage) and is not sent to any server.

5. Data Sharing

User data is NOT shared or sold to third parties. Data may only be shared with authorities when required by law.

8. Children's Privacy
